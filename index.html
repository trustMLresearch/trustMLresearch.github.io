Welcome

The TrustML Young Scientist Seminars (TrustML YSS) is a video series that features young scientists giving talks and discoveries in relation with Trustworthy Machine Learning.

With machine learning techniques increasingly deployed in critical systems, we are at a turning point when we must ensure these techniques are trustworthy and responsible. Towards this goal, TrustML YSS establishes a platform for young scientists to give invited talks about their research related to (but not limited to) robustness, privacy, fairness, explainability, etc.

This network is funded by RIKEN-AIP's subsidy and JST, ACT-X Grant Number JPMJAX21AF, Japan
